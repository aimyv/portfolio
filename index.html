This was the result of my final-year project at the University of Bath. My dissertation revolved around Serious Games and how they could be used to teach mathematical concepts alongside traditional learning methods. Findings from the literature informed the design and implementation of a Serious Game to teach undergraduates about concepts in first-order logic. This was followed by an evaluation into the effectiveness of the game in teaching concepts to new learners and consolidating concepts for experienced learners.
