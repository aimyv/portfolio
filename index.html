Aimy Varghese

Hello! My name is Aimy and I enjoy indoor rock-climbing, listening to podcasts on long walks and most of all, using technology to design solutions that help people!

My interest in technology started when I decided to create a custom theme for my tumblr blog. Some quick googling led me to a Codecademy course on HTML & CSS. From there, I started to experiment with mini-projects and before I knew it, I enrolled to study Computer Science at the University of Bath.

A group project in first year - which involved designing an app to assist diabetics monitor their glucose levels - made me realise a passion for creating projects that helped people. I carried this passion into final year for my dissertation which involved: investigating the use of Serious Games in education, creating my own Serious Game to help undergraduates learn predicate logic, and evaluating the effectiveness of the game I had made.
